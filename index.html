Gilles Villeneuve

Gilles Villeneuve is an iconic figure in the history of F1. He was renowned for his car control, aggressive driving style, and a 'never give up' attitude.

Here is a timeline of Gille Villeneuve's life

  • 1950 - Birth of Gilles Villeneuve.
  • 1953 - Birth of his brother Jacques.
  • 1971 - Birth of his son Jacques.
  • 1972 - His career begins, Gilles joins the Moto-Ski team.
  • 1973 - Receives the title of Rookie of the year and the prestigious title Quebecâ€™s Champion.
  • 1974 - Wins the Snowmobile World Championship and begins in Formula Atlantic series.
  • 1975 - First victory in his Formula Atlantic career at Gimli, Manitoba (Canada).
  • 1976 - Wins 9 out of 10 events in Atlantic Formula, receives the Canadian and American Champion title.
  • 1977 - First race in Formula 1 at Silverstone, United Kingdom. Receives his second Champion title Formula Atlantic and signs at the prestigious Scuderia Ferrari in Formula 1.
  • 1978 - First victory in Formula 1 in Montreal, at the Canadian Grand Prix.
  • 1979 - The F1 victories continue in : South Africa, Long Beach and Watkins Glen.
  • 1981 - Wins at the legendary circuits of Monaco and in Jarama, Spain.
  • 1982 - Fatal accident on May 8th may, during the qualifying session at the Zolder Grand Prix, Belgium.
